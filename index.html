Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Siri Lanka

Dental Surgeon & Family Dentist

Dr. Siri Lanka earned her Bachelor of Dental Surgery from DR. NTR University of Health Sciences, India (2014) and successfully completed the Australian Dental Licensing Examinations in 2023.

Diligent and friendly, Dr. Lanka takes the time to explain each treatment step while ensuring patient comfort throughout. Her special interest is cosmetic dentistry — creating beautiful, functional smiles through ethical, high-quality dental care.

She firmly believes that "a healthy smile is the foundation for a healthy body" and is dedicated to making every dental experience as comfortable and positive as possible.

  • ✔ BDS — DR. NTR University of Health Sciences, India (2014)
  • ✔ Australian Dental Licensing Examinations (2023)
  • ✔ Registered with the New Zealand Dental Council
  • ✔ Member of the New Zealand Dental Association
  • ✔ Special interest in cosmetic & preventive dentistry
