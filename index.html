Meet Dr. Siri Lanka, Dental Surgeon and Family Dentist

After graduating with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery from DR. NTR University in India, Dr. Lanka successfully completed her Australian Dental Licensing Examinations, marking the start of her exciting journey in New Zealand dentistry. Siri's gentle approach and genuine care for her patients ensure that every visit is a comfortable and positive experience. She takes the time to listen to each patient, creating a personalised treatment plan that not only addresses immediate needs but also empowers patients to maintain long-term oral health.

Because Siri understands that a smile is a reflection of confidence, her goal is to help each patient feel proud of theirs. Whether you're visiting for a routine check-up or a more complex procedure, you can count on her to deliver the best possible care with compassion and expertise.

While Siri enjoys all aspects of dentistry, she has a particular passion for cosmetic and preventive dentistry. Driven by a deep passion for patient-centred care, Siri continuously seeks to expand her knowledge and skills through ongoing professional development. She takes pride in creating beautiful, functional smiles, and is committed to providing ethical, high-quality dental care in a gentle and compassionate manner, ensuring every patient feels comfortable and well-informed about their treatment options.

At Shobhasiri Dental, Dr. Lanka's focus is always on delivering the highest standard of care while building trusting, supportive relationships with each patient. Siri looks forward to helping you achieve and maintain the smile you deserve!
